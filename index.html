Party Planner Features

Guest Lists

Once you create your event, you can manage your guest list. Keep track of how many guest you invite and how many people plan on attending. A great way to get a head count as you plan the rest of your party details.

Check Lists

Forgot to buy the streamers? That won't happen with our Shopping and To Do Lists! Create a To Do List to make sure you don't forget to do all those important things before the party day arrives. Ready to buy all the necessary items for your event? Add everything to our Shopping List and mark off each item as they're purchased. It even links to our budget tracker so you can input a price so it keeps track of your spending!

Budget Tracker

Set a budget for each event. We know parties can add up pretty quick. Our budget tracker allows you to set a budget for your event before you begin your shopping. As you purchase things off your shopping list, it will automatically deduct from your party budget, letting you know how much is left to spend.

Entertainment

What's a party without a little entertainment? Whether you decide to rent out a photo booth, hire a DJ or get a clown who makes balloon animals - we've got a place for you to plan your entertainment.

In addition to these features, we also have a Party Mood Board that allows you to add pictures to help inspire you throughout your party planning. See something you like but your party is still six months away? Drop a picture of it on your Party Mood Board so that it's there for you when you're ready. Can't decide on a theme or colors for the parrt? Drop some ideas on to the board so you can compare styles and then make a decision.
